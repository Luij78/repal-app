## Content Categories

### 1. Laws & Rules
- Disciplinary Authority (F.S. 475, F.S. 455, F.S. 120)
- Range of Penalties (reprimand → fine → probation → suspension → revocation)
- Property Management Violations
- F.A.C. 61J2-24 (disciplinary guidelines)

### 2. Key Concepts
- **Max fine per offense:** $5,000
- **Max suspension:** 10 years
- **Sales associate compensation:** ONLY from employing broker
- **Acting as investor:** Must disclose NOT acting as licensee
- **Recovery Fund:** Triggered if licensee misleads public + causes harm

### 3. Violation Flags (for REPal to detect)
- Agent operating property management without broker authorization
- Accepting compensation from non-broker sources
- Misleading public about licensee status while investing
- Acting beyond scope of sales associate
